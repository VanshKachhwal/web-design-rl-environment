Our Story

Born from a love of honest food and bold flavors, we opened our doors in 2012 with a simple mission: serve meals that bring people together. Every dish we craft honors tradition while embracing the unexpected — because great food should never play it safe.

Our Philosophy

Rooted in Craft, Driven by Flavor

We believe that extraordinary meals start long before they reach the plate. It begins with relationships — with the farmers who tend the soil, the foragers who walk the woods at dawn, and the artisans who pour their lives into every wheel of cheese and jar of preserves. We source locally whenever the seasons allow, and we never cut corners on quality.

Our kitchen is a place of experimentation and respect. We honor classic techniques — slow braises, hand-rolled pastas, wood-fired breads — while weaving in unexpected ingredients and global influences. The result is food that feels familiar yet surprising, comforting yet adventurous.

What We Stand For

More Than a Restaurant

Sustainability isn't a buzzword here — it's woven into every decision we make. From composting kitchen scraps to partnering with regenerative farms within a fifty-mile radius, we work to leave a lighter footprint. Our seasonal menu isn't just a creative choice; it's an ecological one.

Community matters just as much. We host monthly supper clubs, donate surplus meals to local shelters, and open our kitchen to aspiring chefs through our apprenticeship program. When you dine with us, you're supporting something bigger than a single meal.

Our Journey

2012

The First Table

We opened with just twelve seats, a wood-fired oven, and a dream. Word spread quickly through the neighborhood, and within months we had a loyal following that felt more like family.

2014

Farm Partnerships

We formalized relationships with six local farms, committing to seasonal sourcing and fair pricing. This partnership transformed our menu and deepened our connection to the land.

2016

The Expansion

Growing demand led us to expand into the adjacent storefront, tripling our seating and adding a dedicated bar program featuring craft cocktails and natural wines.

2019

Apprenticeship Program

We launched our kitchen apprenticeship, offering hands-on training to aspiring chefs from underrepresented backgrounds. Over thirty graduates have gone on to lead their own kitchens.

2021

Zero-Waste Kitchen

After years of incremental improvements, we achieved our zero-waste certification — composting, recycling, and repurposing every scrap that passes through our doors.

2024

A New Chapter

Today we continue to evolve — refreshing our menu each season, mentoring the next generation, and proving that a restaurant can be both delicious and responsible.
