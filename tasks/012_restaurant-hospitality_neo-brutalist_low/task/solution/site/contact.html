Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?
Yes! While we recommend making a reservation — especially on weekends — we always keep a portion of our tables available for walk-in guests on a first-come, first-served basis.
Is there parking available?
We have a small private lot behind the restaurant with 15 spaces. Additional street parking is available along Hearthstone Lane and the surrounding blocks. On busy evenings, we recommend arriving a few minutes early.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely. Our menu includes vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options clearly marked. Please let your server know about any allergies, and our kitchen will be happy to adapt dishes whenever possible.
Do you host private events?
Yes — our private dining room seats up to 40 guests and is perfect for birthdays, corporate dinners, and celebrations. Fill out the form above with "Private Event" as the subject, or call us directly at (503) 555-1234.
How quickly will you respond to my message?
We aim to reply to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters — same-day reservations or last-minute changes — please call us directly.
