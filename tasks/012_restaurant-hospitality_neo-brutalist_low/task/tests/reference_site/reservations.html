Walk-ins are always welcome, subject to availability. During peak hours (Friday and Saturday evenings), we strongly recommend booking in advance to guarantee your preferred time.

Reservation FAQ

How far in advance can I make a reservation?
We accept reservations up to 30 days in advance. For holiday periods and special events, we recommend booking as early as possible as tables fill quickly.
What is your cancellation policy?
We kindly ask that you cancel or modify your reservation at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to accommodate other guests who may be waiting for a table. You can cancel by calling us or sending an email.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely. Our kitchen is experienced with gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, and allergen-conscious preparations. Please note any requirements in the reservation form or inform your server upon arrival. View our full menu for details.
Is there a dress code?
We maintain a smart-casual dress code. We ask that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, flip-flops, or overly casual attire. When in doubt, a collared shirt and closed-toe shoes are always appropriate.
Can I book a private dining room?
Yes! Our private dining room seats up to 20 guests and is perfect for celebrations, corporate dinners, and intimate gatherings. Please contact us to arrange private dining, as these bookings require a custom menu consultation.
What if I'm running late?
We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the scheduled time. If you're running late, please give us a call so we can do our best to accommodate you. After 15 minutes, the table may be released to walk-in guests.
Do you accept walk-ins?
Walk-ins are always welcome on a first-come, first-served basis. However, during peak hours — especially Friday and Saturday evenings — availability may be limited. We recommend reserving ahead to guarantee your spot.

Prefer to Talk to Us?

Our team is happy to help with special requests, large party bookings, or any questions about your upcoming visit. Reach out and we'll take care of everything.
