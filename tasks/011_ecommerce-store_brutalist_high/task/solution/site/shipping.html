Common Questions

Shipping FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most about orders, delivery, and returns.

How do I track my order? +
Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's tracking page. You can also log into your account to view order status at any time. Tracking updates typically appear within 24 hours of shipment.
Do you ship to PO boxes? +
Yes, we ship to PO boxes via Standard and Express shipping methods. Overnight and International shipping require a physical street address for delivery. If you're unsure, please contact us before placing your order.
What if my package arrives damaged? +
We take great care in packaging, but if something arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you. All orders over $50 include shipping insurance.
Can I change my shipping address after ordering? +
Address changes can be accommodated if the order has not yet been packed. Please contact us as soon as possible via our contact page. Once a label has been printed, we are unable to modify the destination address.
Are customs fees included for international orders? +
No, customs duties, import taxes, and brokerage fees are not included in our shipping prices and are the responsibility of the recipient. These charges vary by country and are determined by your local customs authority. We recommend checking with your local postal service for estimates.
What is your return shipping policy? +
We offer free return shipping on all domestic orders within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. International return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer. A prepaid return label will be provided for domestic returns upon request.
Do you offer local pickup? +
Yes, local pickup is available at our workshop. Select "Local Pickup" at checkout and you will receive an email when your order is ready. Pickup hours are Monday through Friday, 10 AM to 5 PM. No shipping charges apply for pickup orders.
