General Questions

About Our Products

Learn more about what we offer, where it comes from, and how we ensure quality in every item.

What types of products do you sell? +
We offer a curated selection of locally sourced goods including handcrafted ceramics, artisan pantry staples, natural textiles, hand-poured candles, and small-batch skincare. Every product is made by independent makers and producers within our regional network. Visit our shop to explore the full catalog.
Are all your products locally made? +
Yes. Every item in our store is sourced from makers and producers within a 250-mile radius. We personally visit each workshop and farm to verify production methods, material sourcing, and working conditions. You can learn more about our sourcing philosophy on our about page.
How do you select your makers and artisans? +
We have a rigorous vetting process that evaluates craftsmanship quality, sustainability practices, material sourcing transparency, and community impact. Each maker undergoes an in-person visit and a trial period before their products are added to our permanent collection. We prioritize long-term partnerships over one-time collaborations.
Do you offer gift wrapping or custom packaging? +
Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping using recycled kraft paper and natural twine. For custom packaging requests or corporate gifting needs, please reach out to us through our contact page and we will work with you to create something special.
Can I see product reviews before purchasing? +
Absolutely. We encourage all customers to leave honest reviews. You can find verified customer feedback on individual product pages or browse our reviews page for a comprehensive overview of what our community has to say about their purchases.
Orders & Shipping

Ordering & Delivery

Details about placing orders, shipping timelines, tracking, and everything related to getting your goods to your door.

How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping typically takes 5–7 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for 2–3 business day delivery. Because many of our items are handmade, some products may require an additional 1–2 days for preparation before shipping. Full details are available on our shipping page.
Do you ship internationally? +
Currently, we ship within the continental United States only. We are actively exploring international shipping partnerships and hope to expand our delivery zones in the near future. Sign up for our newsletter on the home page to be the first to know when international shipping becomes available.
How can I track my order? +
Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a direct link to the carrier's tracking page. If you have not received your tracking information within 3 business days of placing your order, please contact us and we will look into it immediately.
What is your return and exchange policy? +
We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery for items in their original, unused condition. Perishable goods and custom-made items are final sale. To initiate a return, please visit our contact page with your order number and reason for return. We will provide a prepaid return label for defective or incorrect items.
Is there a minimum order amount for free shipping? +
Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all orders over $75. Orders under this amount are charged a flat rate of $6.95 for standard shipping. You can review your shipping options and costs in the cart before completing your purchase. More details on our shipping page.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and digital wallets. All transactions are processed through a secure, encrypted payment gateway. We do not store any credit card information on our servers.
Collections & Products

Product Care & Details

Tips on caring for your handmade goods, understanding materials, and getting the most from your purchase.

How should I care for handmade ceramics? +
Most of our ceramics are food-safe and dishwasher-friendly, though we recommend hand washing to preserve the glaze over time. Avoid sudden temperature changes — do not move ceramics directly from freezer to oven. Each product page includes specific care instructions from the maker. Browse our ceramics in the collections.
Are your products sustainable and eco-friendly? +
Sustainability is at the core of everything we do. Our makers use responsibly sourced materials, minimal packaging, and low-waste production methods wherever possible. Our shipping materials are 100% recyclable or compostable. We provide detailed material and sourcing information on every product page.
Will handmade items have slight variations? +
Yes, and we consider that a feature, not a flaw. Because each item is made by hand, slight variations in color, texture, size, and pattern are natural and expected. These variations are what make each piece unique and authentic. Product photos represent the general appearance, but your item may differ slightly.
Do you restock sold-out items? +
Many of our items are produced in small batches, so availability can be limited. We do restock popular items on a regular basis, but some pieces are one-of-a-kind. We recommend checking our shop frequently or reaching out via our contact page to inquire about specific restocking timelines.
