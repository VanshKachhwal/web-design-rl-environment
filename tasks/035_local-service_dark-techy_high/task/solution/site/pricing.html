Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans.

Q Can I switch plans at any time?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the remaining balance will be credited to your next billing cycle.

Q Is there a free trial available?

Yes, all plans come with a 14-day free trial so you can explore the full feature set before committing. No credit card is required to start your trial.

Q What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. Enterprise customers can also pay via bank transfer or purchase order. Contact our sales team for details.

Q Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes, you save up to 20% when you choose annual billing on any plan. The discount is applied automatically at checkout when you select the yearly option.

Q What happens if I exceed my storage limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your storage limit. You can either upgrade your plan for more storage or purchase additional storage as an add-on. We never delete your data without prior notice.

Q Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel at any time with no cancellation fees. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period. All your data can be exported before cancellation.

Q Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?

Yes, for organizations with unique requirements we offer fully customized pricing and feature packages. Please reach out to our sales team through the contact page to discuss your specific needs.

Q Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance and perform regular third-party security audits. Enterprise plans include additional security features such as SSO and audit logs.
