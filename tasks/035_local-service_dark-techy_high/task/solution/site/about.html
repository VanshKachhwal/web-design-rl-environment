Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Our Journey

Key Milestones

2012

Company Founded

Nexcore was established with a small team of three, driven by a passion for quality craftsmanship and honest service. We took on our first residential projects in the local community.

2015

Expanded Service Areas

Growing demand led us to expand our coverage across the entire metropolitan region, doubling our workforce and investing in specialized equipment and training.

2018

Commercial Division Launched

We opened our dedicated commercial services division, bringing the same attention to detail and reliability to businesses, property managers, and large-scale facilities.

2020

1,000th Project Milestone

We celebrated completing our 1,000th project while maintaining a client satisfaction rate above 97%. This milestone reinforced our commitment to excellence.

2022

Industry Recognition

Nexcore received multiple industry awards for service quality and innovation, solidifying our position as a regional leader in our field.

2024

Digital Transformation

We launched our online booking platform and streamlined client experience, making it easier than ever to schedule services and manage projects digitally.
