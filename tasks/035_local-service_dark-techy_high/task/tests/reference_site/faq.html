Booking

Booking & Scheduling

How to book, reschedule, or cancel appointments with our team.

Q How do I book an appointment?

Booking is easy! Simply visit our Booking page and fill out the form with your preferred date, time, and service details. You can also call or email us directly through our Contact page.

Q How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability. However, we do our best to accommodate same-day and next-day requests when possible. Peak seasons may require longer lead times.

Q Can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment with at least 24 hours' notice at no charge. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee. Please contact us as soon as possible if your plans change.

Q Do you offer weekend or evening appointments?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling including weekends and select evening time slots. Availability varies, so we recommend booking online to see real-time openings that fit your schedule.

Q Will I receive a confirmation after booking?

Yes, you'll receive an email confirmation immediately after your booking is submitted. We'll also send a reminder before your scheduled appointment. If you don't receive a confirmation, please check your spam folder or reach out to us.

Pricing

Pricing & Payment

Transparent information about our rates, payment methods, and billing.

Q How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies depending on the type of service, scope of work, and location. Visit our Pricing page for detailed rate information and package options. We also offer free estimates for larger projects.

Q Do you offer free estimates or quotes?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for most services. You can request a quote through our Contact page or during the booking process. We'll get back to you promptly with a detailed breakdown.

Q What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and cash. Payment is typically collected upon completion of the service. For larger projects, we may arrange a deposit and milestone-based payment schedule.

Q Are there any hidden fees?

Never. We believe in complete transparency. The price quoted is the price you pay. Any additional costs — such as for extra materials or extended scope — will always be discussed and approved by you before we proceed.

Q Do you offer discounts for recurring services?

Yes, we offer discounted rates for clients who sign up for recurring service plans (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly). Check our Pricing page for current promotions and package deals.

Service Details

Service Details

What to expect before, during, and after your service appointment.

Q How long does a typical service take?

Service duration depends on the type and scope of work. Most standard appointments take between 1–3 hours. Larger or more complex projects may require additional time, which we'll communicate upfront during the quoting process.

Q Do I need to be home during the service?

It's not always necessary, but we recommend being available at the start of the appointment to discuss any specific requirements. If you can't be present, we can make arrangements in advance — just let us know when you book.

Q What should I do to prepare for my appointment?

Preparation varies by service type. Generally, we ask that you ensure clear access to the work area and remove any personal valuables. Specific preparation instructions will be included in your booking confirmation email.

Q Do you provide your own equipment and materials?

Yes, we bring all necessary professional-grade equipment and materials. If you have specific product preferences or requirements, please let us know at the time of booking and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Q What if I'm not satisfied with the results?

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely happy with the work, please contact us within 48 hours and we'll arrange a complimentary follow-up visit to address any concerns. We stand behind the quality of our work.
