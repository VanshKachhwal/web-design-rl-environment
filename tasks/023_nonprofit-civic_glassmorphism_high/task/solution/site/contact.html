Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often. Can't find what you're looking for? Use the form above to reach out.

How can I get involved with Wellspring?

There are many ways to contribute! You can volunteer your time, make a financial donation, or explore partnership opportunities. We welcome individuals and organizations of all backgrounds.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

Yes! We gratefully accept in-kind donations of medical supplies, educational materials, and other resources that support our community health programs. Please contact us directly to coordinate drop-off or shipping details.

How do I register for an upcoming event?

Visit our Events page for a full calendar of upcoming workshops, health fairs, and community gatherings. Each event listing includes registration details and any associated costs.

Can I schedule a tour of your facilities?

Absolutely! We love showing visitors our community hub. Please call us at (555) 123-4567 or send a message through the form above to schedule a convenient time. Tours are available Monday through Friday.

How is my donation used?

Transparency is one of our core values. Over 85% of every dollar goes directly to community programs. You can learn more about our allocation and outcomes on our Impact page.

Do you offer programs in languages other than English?

Yes. Many of our programs are offered in Spanish, Mandarin, and Vietnamese. We also provide translation support for other languages upon request. Check our Programs page for details on multilingual offerings.
