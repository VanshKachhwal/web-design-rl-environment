Our Journey of Impact

Key milestones that mark our growth from a small neighborhood initiative to a regional health foundation.

  • 2015

    Foundation Established

    Wellspring Community Health Foundation was incorporated with a mission to bridge health equity gaps in underserved communities. Our first office opened with three staff members and a vision for change.

  • 2017

    First Mobile Clinic Launched

    We deployed our inaugural mobile health unit, bringing free screenings, vaccinations, and preventive care directly to rural communities with limited healthcare access.

  • 2019

    10,000 Patients Milestone

    Reached our first major milestone of 10,000 patients served. Expanded mental health services and launched a chronic disease management program across four counties.

  • 2021

    Pandemic Response Initiative

    Rapidly mobilized resources to deliver COVID-19 testing, vaccination clinics, and telehealth services. Served over 15,000 individuals during the public health emergency.

  • 2023

    Regional Expansion

    Expanded operations to 12 counties with 34 partner organizations. Opened two permanent community health centers and launched youth wellness programs in 18 schools.

  • 2024

    Innovation & Growth

    Introduced data-driven health outcome tracking, surpassed 47,000 patients served, and received national recognition for our community-centered care model.
