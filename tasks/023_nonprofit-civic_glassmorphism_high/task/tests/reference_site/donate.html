At Wellspring Community Health Foundation, fiscal responsibility is a core value. Our lean operational model ensures that the vast majority of every dollar you contribute reaches the people and programs that need it most. We undergo annual independent audits and publish our financial statements for complete donor transparency.

From mobile health clinics serving rural communities to after-school nutrition programs in urban neighborhoods, your generosity directly powers initiatives that measurably improve health outcomes. Every gift, regardless of size, contributes to a healthier, more equitable future for all.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about donating to Wellspring Community Health Foundation.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Wellspring Community Health Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You will receive an official receipt for your records immediately after your contribution is processed.

Can I set up a recurring monthly donation?

Absolutely. Monthly giving is one of the most impactful ways to support our mission. You can choose any giving level as a recurring contribution. Monthly donors receive special impact updates and can adjust or cancel their giving at any time by contacting our team.

Can I donate in honor or memory of someone?

Yes, tribute gifts are a meaningful way to honor a loved one. Simply note the honoree's name when you make your donation, and we will send a personalized acknowledgment card to the person or family you designate. Reach out via our contact page for details.

Does the foundation accept non-monetary donations?

We gratefully accept in-kind donations including medical supplies, hygiene products, educational materials, and professional services. Please visit our contact page or reach out directly so we can coordinate the most effective way to receive your generous contribution.

How can my company get involved?

Corporate partnerships are a vital part of our work. From matching gift programs to sponsored wellness events, there are many ways businesses can make an impact. Visit our partners page to learn about partnership tiers and benefits, or contact us to discuss a custom arrangement.

How do I know my donation is being used effectively?

Transparency is foundational to our work. We publish annual impact reports, undergo independent financial audits, and provide donors at every level with updates on how their funds are being used. Visit our impact page to see our latest results and community outcomes.
