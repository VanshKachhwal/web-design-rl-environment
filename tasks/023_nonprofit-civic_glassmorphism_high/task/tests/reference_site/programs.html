How to Join a Program

Getting started is simple. Follow these four steps to enroll in any of our community health programs.

1

Browse Programs

Explore our full catalog above and find the programs that match your health needs and interests.

2

Check Eligibility

Review program requirements and schedules. Most programs are open to all community members regardless of insurance status.

3

Register

Contact us by phone, email, or visit our office to complete a simple enrollment form. Walk-ins are welcome for most programs.

4

Start Your Journey

Attend your first session and connect with our caring staff. We'll support you every step of the way toward better health.
