Why Volunteer

Your Time Transforms Lives

Volunteering with Wellspring Community Health Foundation is more than service — it's a chance to connect deeply with your community, develop new skills, and witness firsthand the impact of collective action. Whether you have a few hours a month or want to commit weekly, there's a meaningful role waiting for you.

Our volunteers report greater personal fulfillment, stronger social connections, and a renewed sense of purpose. You'll work alongside dedicated staff and fellow volunteers who share your passion for community health.

How to Get Started

Becoming a Wellspring volunteer is simple. Follow these four steps and you'll be making a difference in no time.

1

Submit Application

Fill out our volunteer application form below with your interests, availability, and any relevant experience. We welcome all skill levels.

2

Attend Orientation

Join a welcome session where you'll learn about our mission, meet staff, and discover the programs that align with your passions.

3

Complete Training

Receive role-specific training to prepare you for success. Our comprehensive onboarding ensures you feel confident and supported.

4

Start Serving

Begin your volunteer journey with a team that values your contribution. Ongoing mentorship and community keep you connected.
