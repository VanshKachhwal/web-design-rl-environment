My Story

From Curiosity to Craft

My journey began with a fascination for how things work beneath the surface. What started as tinkering with code in a dimly lit room evolved into a career dedicated to building robust, elegant systems that solve real-world problems. I believe great software lives at the intersection of technical excellence and human empathy.

  • Full-stack development with modern frameworks
  • User-centered design and accessibility advocacy
  • Open-source contributor and community mentor
  • Continuous learner and knowledge sharer
Philosophy

What I Believe In

Every line of code is a decision. I strive to make each one intentional, maintainable, and in service of the people who will ultimately use what I build.

Quality Over Quantity

I believe in shipping fewer features that work beautifully rather than many that work partially. Every interaction should feel considered and polished.

Accessibility First

The web is for everyone. I design and develop with inclusivity at the forefront, ensuring that no user is left behind regardless of ability or device.

Systems Thinking

I approach problems holistically, building scalable design systems and architectures that grow gracefully with evolving requirements and team needs.

Collaborative Spirit

The best work emerges from diverse perspectives. I thrive in cross-functional teams and value open communication, code reviews, and shared ownership.
