Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my pass after purchase?

Yes! You can upgrade from a Day Pass to a Full Pass or VIP Pass at any time before the event. Simply reach out to our team via the contact page and we'll apply the price difference to your upgrade.

What is the refund and cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Between 30 and 14 days, a 50% refund is issued. Within 14 days of the event, tickets are non-refundable but may be transferred to another attendee at no cost.

Are group discounts available?

Absolutely. Groups of 5 or more receive 15% off, and groups of 10 or more receive 25% off. Contact us through the contact page to arrange group pricing.

Is there a student or early-career discount?

We offer a 30% student discount on Full Pass and Day Pass tickets with a valid student ID. Early-career professionals within 2 years of graduation also qualify. Reach out via our contact form with proof of eligibility.

What does the wellness kit include?

The Starter kit includes a branded tote bag, journal, and essential oil sampler. The Premium kit adds a meditation cushion and herbal tea collection. The Deluxe VIP kit includes all of the above plus a premium yoga mat and personalized wellness plan consultation.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes, all ticket types are transferable up to 48 hours before the event. Simply contact our team with the new attendee's details and we'll update the registration.

Where is the event held?

WellnessConX 2025 takes place at the Harmony Convention Center. Visit our venue page for full details including directions, parking, and nearby accommodations.

Will sessions be recorded?

Full Pass and VIP Pass holders receive access to post-event recordings of all keynotes and select workshops. Recordings are typically available within 2 weeks after the conference concludes.
