Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for WellnessConX 2025?
You can register directly through our Tickets page. We offer several tier options including Early Bird, Standard, and VIP passes. All registrations include access to the main conference sessions.
What is the refund policy for tickets?
Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Between 30 and 14 days, a 50% refund is issued. Within 14 days of the event, tickets are non-refundable but may be transferred to another attendee. Contact us at hello@wellnessconx.com for transfer requests.
Can I submit a speaker proposal?
Absolutely! We welcome speaker submissions from wellness professionals, researchers, and thought leaders. Please use the contact form above and select "Speaker Proposals" as your subject. Include a brief bio and your proposed topic. You can also browse our current speaker lineup.
Is the venue accessible?
Yes, the Wellness Convention Center is fully ADA-compliant with wheelchair-accessible entrances, elevators, restrooms, and seating areas. ASL interpretation and assistive listening devices are available upon request. Visit our Venue page for full details.
Are there sponsorship opportunities available?
Yes! We offer multiple sponsorship tiers with varying levels of visibility and engagement. Visit our Sponsors page for details on current partners and available packages, or reach out through the form above.
What is the typical response time for inquiries?
Our team responds to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call us directly at +1 (555) 123-4567 during office hours (Mon–Fri, 9 AM – 6 PM CST).
Where can I find the event schedule?
The full conference schedule, including keynotes, workshops, breakout sessions, and networking events, is available on our Schedule page. The schedule is regularly updated as we confirm additional sessions and speakers.
