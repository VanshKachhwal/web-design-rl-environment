Premier Location

The Grand Wellness Pavilion

Nestled in the heart of downtown, the Grand Wellness Pavilion offers 120,000 square feet of flexible event space across three levels. With floor-to-ceiling windows flooding every hall with natural light, biophilic design elements throughout, and state-of-the-art acoustics, this venue was crafted for transformative experiences.

Surrounded by lush gardens and reflective water features, attendees can step outside between sessions to recharge in nature — a true extension of the wellness philosophy that drives WellnessConX.

Amenities

Everything You Need, On-Site

The venue features dedicated meditation rooms, a rooftop yoga terrace, organic catering stations, hydration bars, and wellness lounges with massage chairs. High-speed Wi-Fi blankets every corner, and our accessibility-first design ensures every attendee feels welcome.

On-site parking accommodates 2,000 vehicles, with EV charging stations and complimentary shuttle service from partner hotels within a 5-mile radius.

Getting Here

Whether you're arriving by air, rail, or road, the Grand Wellness Pavilion is easy to reach from anywhere.

By Air

The venue is just 12 miles from the International Airport. Complimentary shuttle service runs every 30 minutes from Terminal 2 arrivals during conference days.

By Train

Central Station is a 10-minute walk from the pavilion. Direct rail links connect major cities with frequent service throughout the day.

By Car

Located right off Highway 101, Exit 24B. On-site parking for 2,000 vehicles with 50 EV charging stations. Valet parking available for VIP pass holders.

Accommodation

We've partnered with nearby hotels to offer exclusive rates for WellnessConX attendees. Book early for the best availability.

The Serenity Hotel — 0.2 miles from venue. Exclusive conference rate available. Features an on-site spa, rooftop pool, and farm-to-table restaurant. Complimentary breakfast included for all WellnessConX guests.

Harmony Suites — 0.5 miles from venue. All-suite accommodations with kitchenettes, ideal for extended stays. Shuttle service provided every 15 minutes during conference hours.

The Mindful Inn — 0.8 miles from venue. Budget-friendly option with modern amenities, co-working spaces, and a wellness center. Walking distance through the scenic Pavilion Park trail.

For booking codes and special rates, visit the contact page or reach out to our concierge team directly. Group bookings of 10+ rooms receive additional discounts.
