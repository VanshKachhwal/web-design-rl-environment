// Culture

How We Work Together

We don't believe in silos. At Vandal, designers code, developers critique layouts, and strategists sketch wireframes. The best ideas emerge when disciplines collide — when someone from engineering challenges a design assumption, or a copywriter reshapes an entire user flow.

Our studio runs on radical candor, shared ownership, and a deep respect for craft. We hire slowly, trust fully, and give every person the autonomy to do the best work of their career. No open-plan theater. No performative hustle. Just focused, intentional collaboration.

We're remote-first but never remote-only. Quarterly summits, async-first communication, and a culture of documentation keep us aligned without the overhead of constant meetings.

// Values

What We Stand For

These aren't wall art. They're the operating principles that guide every decision, every hire, and every deliverable.

Craft Over Convenience

We never take shortcuts. Every detail is deliberate, every decision is defended, and every deliverable reflects our highest standard.

Radical Transparency

No hidden agendas, no politics. We share context openly, give feedback directly, and trust each other to handle the truth.

Global Perspective

Our distributed team brings diverse viewpoints to every project. Different backgrounds lead to better solutions and more resonant work.

Ownership Mentality

Every team member owns their domain completely. We don't micromanage — we hire exceptional people and give them the space to prove it.

Bias Toward Action

We prototype before we present. We ship before we perfect. Momentum matters — and we'd rather iterate on something real than debate something theoretical.

Client as Partner

We don't work for clients — we work with them. The best outcomes happen when there's mutual trust, shared risk, and a common definition of success.
