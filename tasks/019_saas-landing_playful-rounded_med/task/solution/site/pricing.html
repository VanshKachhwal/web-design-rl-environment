FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans.

Q Can I switch plans at any time?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing period.

Q Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes! Every new account gets a 14-day free trial of our Pro plan with all features unlocked. No credit card required to start. At the end of the trial, you can choose to continue with Pro, upgrade to Enterprise, or switch to our free Starter plan.

Q What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. For Enterprise plans, we also offer invoicing with net-30 payment terms. All payments are processed securely.

Q Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes, we offer a 20% discount when you choose annual billing. That means Pro comes to just $23/month and Enterprise to $79/month when billed annually. Contact our sales team for custom annual agreements on Enterprise plans.

Q What happens if I exceed my storage limit?

We'll notify you when you reach 80% and 95% of your storage limit. You won't lose any data if you go over — we'll simply ask you to upgrade your plan or clean up unused files. We never delete your data without your permission.

Q Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days in case you change your mind.

Q Do you offer special pricing for nonprofits or education?

We do! We offer a 50% discount for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Please reach out to our team through the contact page with your organization details and we'll get you set up.
