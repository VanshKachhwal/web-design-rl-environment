Our Journey

From Idea to Impact

Key milestones that have shaped Circl into the platform thousands of teams rely on today.

January 2019

The Spark

Two former colleagues frustrated by fragmented workflows sketch the first wireframes of Circl in a coffee shop. The core idea: one unified space where conversations, tasks, and files live together.

August 2019

Seed Funding Secured

Raised our first round of funding, allowing us to hire our founding engineering team of five. We moved into our first real office — a converted garage with questionable Wi-Fi.

March 2020

Beta Launch

Released our private beta to 200 early adopters. The feedback was overwhelming — both the praise and the bug reports. We shipped 47 patches in the first month.

November 2020

Public Launch

Circl went live for everyone. Within the first week, over 2,000 teams signed up. Remote work was the new normal, and our timing couldn't have been better.

June 2021

Integrations Ecosystem

Launched our integrations marketplace with 30+ partners, connecting Circl to the tools teams already love. Adoption doubled within three months.

February 2022

Series A & Global Expansion

Closed our Series A funding round. Opened offices in London and Singapore, growing the team to 85 people across 12 countries.

October 2023

10,000 Teams Milestone

Crossed the 10,000 active teams mark. Launched enterprise-grade security features and SOC 2 Type II certification to serve larger organizations.

2024 — Today

What's Next

We're investing heavily in intelligent workflows, deeper analytics, and making Circl the most intuitive collaboration platform on the planet. The best is yet to come.

Our Culture

How We Work Together

We're a remote-first company that values autonomy, deep work, and genuine human connection.

At Circl, we practice what we preach. Our own team relies on the platform every day to collaborate across time zones, share ideas asynchronously, and keep projects moving forward without unnecessary meetings.

Remote-First, Not Remote-Only

While our team spans 12 countries, we maintain strong connections through intentional rituals: weekly all-hands, quarterly off-sites, and a culture of writing things down so everyone stays in the loop regardless of when they log on.

Continuous Learning

Every team member receives a dedicated learning budget and time for professional development. We host internal tech talks, design critiques, and book clubs. Growth isn't just encouraged — it's expected.

Work-Life Harmony

We trust our team to manage their time and energy. Flexible schedules, generous time off, and a no-guilt policy around disconnecting are core to how we operate. We measure outcomes, not hours logged.

  • Flexible working hours with core overlap windows
  • Annual learning and development stipend
  • Quarterly team retreats in rotating locations
  • Comprehensive health and wellness benefits
  • Home office setup allowance for every new hire

Interested in joining us? We're always looking for talented people who share our values. Check out our open positions or reach out — we'd love to hear from you.
