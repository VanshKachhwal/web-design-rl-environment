Author Spotlight

Sarah Mitchell

Lead Technology Editor — Exploring the intersection of code, culture, and learning

Sarah Mitchell joined EduPulse in 2020 after a decade of building enterprise software systems at leading technology companies. Her transition from engineering to education was driven by a simple belief: everyone deserves to understand the technology shaping their world.

Her writing spans artificial intelligence, cloud infrastructure, web development, and digital literacy. Sarah is known for her ability to break down intimidating technical concepts into clear, step-by-step explanations that resonate with beginners and experienced practitioners alike.

Beyond her articles, Sarah mentors aspiring tech writers and leads EduPulse's editorial standards for technical accuracy. She holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from Stanford University and is a frequent speaker at education technology conferences.
