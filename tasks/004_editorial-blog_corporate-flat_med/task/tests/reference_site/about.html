Our Story

Born From a Passion for Knowledge

EduPulse started in 2018 as a small blog run by a group of university educators frustrated by the gap between academic research and practical classroom insights. What began as a side project quickly grew into a thriving community of writers, thinkers, and lifelong learners.

Today, we publish in-depth articles across dozens of topics, curated by expert authors who share a common mission: making quality educational content freely available to everyone.

  • Founded by educators with 50+ years of combined experience
  • Peer-reviewed articles ensuring accuracy and depth
  • Community-driven topics shaped by reader feedback
  • 100% free — no paywalls, no subscriptions required
Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Research and Practice

Too often, groundbreaking educational research remains locked away in academic journals. Our mission is to translate complex findings into actionable, readable content that teachers, students, and curious minds can apply immediately.

We partner with researchers, practitioners, and thought leaders across disciplines to ensure every article we publish meets the highest standards of clarity and rigor.

Our Journey

Milestones Along the Way

From a humble blog to a trusted educational resource — here's how EduPulse has grown over the years.

January 2018

EduPulse Is Founded

Three university professors launch EduPulse as a weekend project, publishing their first five articles on pedagogy and classroom innovation.

September 2019

10,000 Monthly Readers

Organic growth and word-of-mouth referrals help EduPulse reach its first major readership milestone, with content spanning 12 educational topics.

March 2020

Remote Learning Series Launch

In response to the global shift to online education, EduPulse publishes a comprehensive series on remote teaching strategies, reaching educators in 40+ countries.

June 2021

Author Network Expands

EduPulse opens its platform to guest contributors, growing the author network to over 50 expert writers across education, technology, and science.

November 2022

Featured Content Program

Launch of the Featured Content program, highlighting the most impactful and well-researched articles each month, as selected by our editorial board.

January 2024

500+ Published Articles

EduPulse celebrates publishing over 500 articles, with a growing community of 100,000+ monthly readers and contributors from six continents.

Our Values

What We Stand For

These core principles guide every article we publish, every author we welcome, and every decision we make.

Accessibility First

Education should never be gated. All EduPulse content is free to read, share, and use in classrooms around the world. No paywalls, no exceptions.

Rigor & Accuracy

Every article undergoes editorial review for factual accuracy, proper sourcing, and clarity. We hold ourselves to the same standards as academic publications.

Global Perspective

Our authors and readers span six continents. We actively seek diverse viewpoints to ensure our content reflects the richness of global education.

Practical Application

Theory is only valuable when it can be applied. We focus on translating research into strategies, tools, and frameworks that educators can use today.

Community Driven

Our readers shape what we publish. Through feedback, topic suggestions, and open dialogue, the EduPulse community is at the heart of everything we do.

Continuous Growth

Learning never stops — and neither do we. We continuously refine our editorial process, expand our topic coverage, and improve the reader experience.
