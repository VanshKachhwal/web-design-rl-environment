Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive. Can't find what you're looking for? Scroll down to contact us directly.

Q How do I get started with the platform?

Head over to our Guides page for a comprehensive getting-started tutorial. It walks you through installation, initial configuration, and building your first project in under ten minutes.

Q Where can I find the full API documentation?

Our complete API documentation is available on the Reference page. It includes detailed descriptions of every endpoint, request and response schemas, authentication methods, and rate-limiting policies.

Q Is there a free tier or trial available?

Yes! We offer a generous free tier that includes access to all core features. No credit card is required to get started. You can explore the platform at your own pace before deciding on a paid plan.

Q How do I report a bug or request a feature?

You can report bugs or submit feature requests by using the contact form below. Please include as much detail as possible — steps to reproduce, expected behavior, and any relevant screenshots or code snippets help us respond faster.

Q What programming languages and frameworks are supported?

We provide official client libraries for JavaScript, Python, Ruby, Go, and Java. Community-maintained libraries exist for several other languages. Visit our Examples page to see integration patterns for popular frameworks.

Q What is the typical response time for support requests?

We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 24 hours on business days. Critical issues are prioritized and typically receive a response within 4 hours. Paid plans include expedited support with guaranteed SLAs.

Q Can I migrate from another platform?

Absolutely. We provide migration guides and tooling to help you transition smoothly. Our Guides section includes a dedicated migration walkthrough, and our support team is available to assist with complex migrations.
