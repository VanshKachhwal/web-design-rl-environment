About Us

Built for builders, by builders

We started with a simple belief: teams deserve tools that stay out of their way. Since 2018, we've been crafting software that empowers organizations to move faster, collaborate better, and ship with confidence.

What began as a small project between three engineers frustrated with bloated workflows has grown into a platform trusted by thousands of teams worldwide. We remain independent, profitable, and obsessively focused on our users.

Our headquarters sit in a converted warehouse where design, engineering, and support work side by side — no silos, no politics, just a shared commitment to making great software. Every feature we ship is something we use ourselves, every day.

Our Journey

2018

Founded in a Garage

Three engineers left their corporate roles to build the workflow tool they always wished existed. The first prototype launched within 90 days.

2019

First 1,000 Users

Word of mouth drove organic growth. We crossed 1,000 active users without spending a dollar on marketing, validating the product-market fit we believed in.

2020

Remote-First Pivot

As the world shifted to distributed work, our collaboration features became essential. User growth tripled and we expanded the team to 15 people.

2021

Enterprise Launch

We introduced SSO, audit logs, and advanced permissions — opening the door to enterprise clients. Fortune 500 companies began adopting the platform.

2023

Platform Expansion

Launched integrations marketplace, API v3, and a redesigned interface. Surpassed 10,000 active teams across 40 countries.

2024

The Road Ahead

With a growing team of 45 and a thriving community, we're doubling down on performance, accessibility, and the features our users request most.

Our Values

What drives everything we do

These aren't words on a wall — they're the principles we use to make decisions every single day, from product roadmap to hiring.

Ship with intent. Every feature must solve a real problem. We measure success by outcomes, not output. If it doesn't make our users' lives meaningfully better, it doesn't ship.

Default to transparency. Our roadmap is public, our pricing is straightforward, and our team communicates openly — internally and externally. Trust is earned through clarity.

Stay lean, stay fast. We're intentionally small. A focused team with clear ownership moves faster than a sprawling organization with layers of approval. Speed is a feature.

Respect the craft. Great software is a blend of engineering rigor and design empathy. We care about the details — from API response times to button padding — because our users notice.
