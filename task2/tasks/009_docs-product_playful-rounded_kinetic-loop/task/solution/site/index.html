Core Features

Everything your docs workflow needs

From first draft to published page, CloudNest handles the entire documentation lifecycle with tools your team will love.

✎

Collaborative Editor

Real-time co-editing with inline comments, suggestions, and version history. No more emailing Word files back and forth.

⚡

Instant Publishing

Push changes live in under two seconds. Our edge network across 12 EU regions ensures blazing-fast page loads for every reader.

🔒

GDPR by Design

Data never leaves the EU. Full audit trails, data processing agreements, and privacy controls are built into every plan.

⌥

API-First Architecture

Every feature is accessible via our REST and GraphQL APIs. Automate publishing, manage content, and integrate with your stack.

🔍

Semantic Search

AI-powered search understands intent, not just keywords. Your readers find answers in seconds, not minutes.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

See which pages get read, where readers drop off, and what's missing. Data-driven docs that improve over time.

How It Works

From zero to published in three steps

1

Connect your repo

Link a GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket repository. CloudNest watches for changes and syncs automatically.

2

Write & review

Use Markdown, MDX, or our visual editor. Invite reviewers, leave comments, and approve changes before they go live.

3

Publish globally

One click deploys to our EU edge network. Custom domains, versioning, and access controls are ready out of the box.
