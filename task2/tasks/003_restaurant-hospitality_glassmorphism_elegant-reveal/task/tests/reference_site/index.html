Welcome

Where Fire Meets the Vine

Nestled on the corner of Elm and Fourth, Ember & Vine has been a gathering place for our community since 2017. Chef Margaux Soleil and her team craft menus that shift with the seasons — drawing from farms within thirty miles and vineyards that share our passion for honest, expressive flavors.

Whether you're here for a weeknight glass of natural wine at the bar or a slow Saturday supper with family, every visit is designed to feel like coming home.

What Defines Us

Three Pillars of Our Table

🔥

Live-Fire Cooking

Every dish passes through our open hearth — wood-roasted, ember-kissed, and finished with care over oak and cherry wood.

🌿

Seasonal & Local

Our menu changes monthly, guided by what's ripening at Hollow Creek Farm, Stone Gate Dairy, and the gardens we tend ourselves.

🍷

Curated Wines

Over 120 labels — small-production, organic, and biodynamic — selected by sommelier David Ashworth to pair with every course.
