Before You Visit

Cancellation Policy

We kindly ask for 24-hour notice for cancellations or changes. Late cancellations may incur a $25 per-person fee for parties of four or more.

Dietary Accommodations

Our kitchen happily accommodates vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and most allergy needs. Please note any requirements when booking or speak with your server.

Accessibility

Ember & Vine is fully wheelchair accessible with step-free entry, accessible restrooms, and accommodating seating. Service animals are always welcome.

Parking

Complimentary valet parking is available Friday and Saturday evenings. A public lot sits directly behind the restaurant on Vine Street, and metered street parking is free after 7:00 PM. Bicycle racks are located at our front entrance.

Dress Code

We embrace smart-casual attire — think well-fitted jeans with a blazer, a comfortable dress, or your favorite pair of boots. We want you to feel at home while savoring something special. No shorts or flip-flops, please.

Send Us a Message

Questions about our menu, private events, gift cards, or anything else? Drop us a line and we'll respond within one business day.
