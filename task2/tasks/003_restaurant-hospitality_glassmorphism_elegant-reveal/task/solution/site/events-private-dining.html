Good to Know

Policies & Details

Minimums & Pricing

The Hearth Room requires a food & beverage minimum of $2,400 (Friday–Saturday) or $1,600 (Sunday–Thursday). The Cellar Alcove has no minimum on weeknights; a $900 minimum applies on weekends. All pricing excludes tax and 20% service charge.

Booking & Deposits

We recommend booking 3–6 weeks in advance for private dining and 2+ months for holiday events. A 25% deposit secures your date, with the balance due 48 hours before the event. Cancellations within 7 days forfeit the deposit.

Dietary Accommodations

Every private menu can be adapted for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and common allergy needs. Please share dietary requirements at least one week prior so our kitchen can prepare thoughtfully.

Décor & Extras

We provide candles, linens, and simple floral arrangements at no extra charge. You're welcome to bring custom florals, signage, or favors — just coordinate with your event contact. Cake cutting service is available for $4 per person.
