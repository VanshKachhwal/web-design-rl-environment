Frequently Asked Questions

Are all resources really free?

Yes. Every toolkit, template, and guide on EduPulse is available at no cost. We believe high-quality teaching resources should be accessible to every educator, regardless of school budget.

Can I modify the templates for my school?

Absolutely. All editable documents are provided in open formats. We encourage you to adapt them to your curriculum standards, student demographics, and institutional requirements.

How often are new resources published?

We release two to three new resources each week, with major toolkit updates every quarter. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay current with every release.

I'd like to contribute a resource. How can I?

We welcome educator contributions! Visit our contact page and select "Resource Submission" to share your materials with our editorial team for review.

Do you offer resources for higher education faculty?

Yes. While many resources are designed for K–12, our assessment, engagement, and professional growth categories include materials specifically tailored for college and university instructors.
