About EduPulse

Built by Educators, for Everyone Who Learns

EduPulse started in 2021 as a small newsletter between three college professors who wanted to share evidence-based teaching practices. Today, we're a growing editorial platform with contributors from over 40 universities worldwide.

Our mission is simple: make the science of learning accessible, practical, and free for every student and educator.
