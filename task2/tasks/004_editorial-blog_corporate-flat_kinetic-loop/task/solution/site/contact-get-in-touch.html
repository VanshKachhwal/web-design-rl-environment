Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to reply to every message within 24 hours on business days. Partnership inquiries may take up to 48 hours as they go through our editorial review team.

Can I write for EduPulse as a student?

Absolutely! We actively encourage student voices. Send us a 200-word pitch along with a writing sample, and our editors will review it within a week.

Do you offer advertising or sponsored content?

We offer sponsored articles, newsletter placements, and sidebar features for education-aligned brands. Select "Partnership Opportunity" in the form above for details.

Is there a way to receive your articles by email?

Yes! Subscribe to our weekly digest on the homepage and get curated articles delivered every Tuesday morning.

I'm an educator — do you have bulk resources?

Check out our Resources for Educators page for downloadable guides, lesson frameworks, and classroom toolkits.
