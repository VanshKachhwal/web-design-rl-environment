How Partnership Works

A streamlined four-step process from initial outreach to full deployment — designed for government timelines and nonprofit workflows.

01

Discovery Call

We assess your jurisdiction's compliance landscape, data systems, and community needs in a 30-minute consultation.

02

Data Mapping

Our team maps your permit databases, tax incentive programs, and regulatory frameworks into CivicCore's compliance engine.

03

Pilot Launch

A 90-day pilot program with a subset of local businesses validates accuracy, usability, and community impact metrics.

04

Full Deployment

Rollout to your entire network with dedicated support, co-branded dashboards, and ongoing data synchronization.
