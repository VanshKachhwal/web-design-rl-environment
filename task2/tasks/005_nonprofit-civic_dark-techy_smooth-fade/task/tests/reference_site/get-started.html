// faq

Common Questions

Quick answers to help you get started with confidence.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

Not at all. CivicCore is designed for business owners, not developers. The terminal aesthetic is just our design style — the interface is fully point-and-click.

What happens after the free tier?

Your free account stays active indefinitely with one jurisdiction. Upgrade only when you need multi-jurisdiction coverage or advanced features like tax incentive matching.

How current is the compliance data?

Our engine syncs with government databases daily. Critical regulatory changes trigger real-time alerts for Professional and Enterprise users.

Can I add team members?

Professional plans support up to 3 team seats. Enterprise plans include unlimited seats with role-based access controls and audit logging.

Is my business data secure?

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and never share data with third parties.

What if my jurisdiction isn't covered?

We cover all 50 U.S. states and 3,200+ counties. If a specific municipality is missing, submit a request and we typically add it within 5 business days.
