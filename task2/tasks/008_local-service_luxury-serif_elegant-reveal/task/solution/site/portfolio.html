Project Spotlight

The Ashford Estate

A comprehensive eighteen-month restoration of a 1927 Georgian Revival estate in Greenwich, Connecticut. Our team meticulously restored original plaster crown moldings, rebuilt the grand staircase using reclaimed white oak, and modernized all mechanical systems while preserving every architectural detail that defined the home's historic character.

Scope: 12,400 sq ft  ·  Duration: 18 months  ·  Year: 2023

How We Work

Our Renovation Process

Every Sterling project follows a disciplined methodology refined over two decades, ensuring exceptional results from initial vision through final reveal.

01

Discovery & Vision

We begin with an immersive consultation at your property, studying its architectural DNA, understanding your aspirations, and documenting every detail that will inform the design direction.

02

Design Development

Our design team creates comprehensive plans with material palettes, detailed renderings, and specification documents. We present options ranging from historically faithful to thoughtfully contemporary.

03

Artisan Execution

Master craftsmen bring the vision to life with meticulous attention to joinery, finishes, and proportion. Weekly progress reviews ensure alignment with your expectations at every stage.

04

The Reveal

A curated walkthrough of your transformed space, followed by a comprehensive care guide and our lifetime craftsmanship warranty that stands behind every detail we deliver.

Project Spotlight

The Kensington Project

A landmark reimagination of a five-story Upper East Side brownstone, blending its 1889 Romanesque Revival bones with refined contemporary interiors. We restored the original sandstone façade, reconstructed period-appropriate cornices, and opened the interior with a dramatic skylit atrium that floods all five floors with natural light.

Scope: 7,200 sq ft  ·  Duration: 14 months  ·  Year: 2024
