Signature Service

Heritage Estate Restoration

Our most sought-after offering, heritage estate restoration requires a rare combination of historical knowledge, artisan craftsmanship, and modern engineering. We work with properties dating from the early 1800s through mid-century, carefully documenting original details before beginning any intervention.

Every restoration includes a comprehensive archival report, hand-matched material sourcing, and coordination with preservation specialists when required. The result is a home that feels both timeless and effortlessly livable.

Our Approach

The Sterling Process

A disciplined, transparent methodology refined over two decades of serving discerning homeowners. Every project follows six deliberate phases.

01

Discovery & Consultation

We visit your property, listen to your aspirations, study the existing architecture, and develop a clear understanding of scope, timeline, and investment parameters.

02

Design & Documentation

Our design team produces detailed architectural drawings, material palettes, and 3D renderings. You approve every specification before a single tool is lifted.

03

Material Sourcing

We source stone, timber, hardware, and fixtures from trusted suppliers across North America and Europe—prioritizing authenticity, durability, and provenance.

04

Precision Construction

Our in-house craftsmen and vetted trade partners execute the build with meticulous attention. Weekly progress reports and site walks keep you informed at every stage.

05

Quality Assurance

A rigorous 200-point inspection checklist ensures every joint, finish, and system meets Sterling standards before we consider a project complete.

06

Handover & Stewardship

We deliver a comprehensive homeowner's manual, walk you through every detail, and remain available for ongoing maintenance guidance and future enhancements.
