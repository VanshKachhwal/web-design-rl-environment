Our Story

A Legacy of Refined
Craftsmanship

For over three decades, Sterling & Associates has elevated the art of residential renovation — restoring heritage estates and reimagining luxury homes with the precision and reverence they deserve.

Since 1991

Founded on Principle,
Built on Trust

Sterling & Associates was established by Richard Sterling in Greenwich, Connecticut, with a singular conviction: that the homes of discerning families deserve the same caliber of artistry once reserved for grand public architecture. What began as a boutique restoration practice has grown into North America's most sought-after residential renovation firm.

Today, under the continued leadership of the Sterling family and a hand-selected team of master craftspeople, architects, and conservators, we steward properties ranging from Gilded Age mansions to contemporary waterfront estates — always with an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Guiding Principles

The Values That Define Us

Every decision we make — from the selection of a single hinge to the orchestration of a full estate restoration — is guided by principles that have endured since our founding.

Uncompromising Quality

We source the finest materials — reclaimed heart pine, hand-forged ironwork, imported marble — and pair them with techniques perfected over generations. Shortcuts are simply not in our vocabulary.

Reverence for Heritage

Whether restoring a 1920s Tudor or preserving the original millwork of a Federal-style townhouse, we honor the architectural intent of every era while thoughtfully integrating modern comfort.

Absolute Discretion

Our clients include prominent families, public figures, and collectors who trust us with their most personal spaces. Confidentiality and respect for privacy are foundational to every engagement.

Our Approach

Where Vision Meets
Meticulous Execution

Every Sterling project begins with deep listening. We spend weeks understanding not just the architecture, but the rhythms of your life within it — how light moves through your morning room, which hallway your children race through, where you retreat at the end of a long day.

This intimate understanding informs a bespoke renovation plan that balances preservation with innovation, delivered through a single point of contact who orchestrates every artisan, supplier, and timeline with quiet authority.

The Sterling Process

From First Conversation
to Final Detail

Our proven methodology ensures that every renovation unfolds with grace, transparency, and an absolute commitment to your vision.

01

Discovery & Assessment

We conduct a comprehensive evaluation of your property — its history, structural integrity, and unrealized potential — alongside an in-depth conversation about your aspirations.

02

Design & Documentation

Our architects and historians develop detailed plans that honor original character while incorporating modern systems, materials, and spatial flow.

03

Master Craftsmanship

Our in-house artisans and vetted specialists execute every element — from hand-plastered ceilings to custom cabinetry — with museum-grade precision.

04

Reveal & Stewardship

We present your transformed home with a comprehensive care guide and remain available for ongoing maintenance, ensuring your investment endures for generations.
