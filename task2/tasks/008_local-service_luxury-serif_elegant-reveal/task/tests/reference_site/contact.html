Your Journey

What Happens After You Reach Out

From first contact to final walkthrough, our process is designed to honor your time, protect your investment, and deliver results that endure for generations.

01

Personal Response

Within 24 hours, a senior consultant reviews your inquiry and contacts you directly — never a call center, never an assistant. We listen before we propose.

02

On-Site Discovery

We visit your property to understand the architecture, the light, the materials, and — most importantly — how you live. This complimentary assessment typically lasts 60 to 90 minutes.

03

Design Concept & Proposal

Our team presents a tailored design direction with material palettes, spatial plans, and a transparent investment outline — so you can move forward with complete confidence.

04

Craftsmanship Begins

Once approved, our master artisans and trusted trade partners bring the vision to life with weekly progress updates, rigorous quality inspections, and unwavering attention to detail.

Common Questions

Before You Contact Us

What is your minimum project size?

We typically engage on projects with a construction budget of $250,000 or more. This ensures we can dedicate the level of design leadership and artisan oversight that defines our work.

Do you work outside the New York metropolitan area?

Absolutely. With studios in Greenwich, Palm Beach, and Toronto, we serve clients across North America. For estates beyond our regional offices, we maintain a dedicated travel team for projects of exceptional scope.

How long does a typical renovation take?

Timelines vary with complexity. A kitchen and bath redesign may require 4 to 6 months, while a full estate restoration can span 12 to 24 months. We provide a detailed schedule before any work begins.

Can you work with our existing architect or designer?

We welcome collaboration. Many of our most celebrated projects have involved partnerships with independent architects, landscape designers, and interior decorators. Seamless coordination is one of our core strengths.

Is the initial consultation complimentary?

Yes. The on-site discovery visit and preliminary conversation carry no obligation. We believe the relationship must feel right for both parties before any commitment is made.
