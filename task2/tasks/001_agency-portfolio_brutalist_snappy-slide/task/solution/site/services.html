Methodology

How We Work

Every engagement follows a four-phase structure designed to reduce risk, maintain velocity, and ensure alignment from kickoff to launch and beyond.

Phase 01

Discover

Stakeholder interviews, technical audits, competitive analysis, and user research. We define the problem space before proposing solutions.

2 – 3 Weeks

Phase 02

Define

Information architecture, system mapping, wireframes, and technical specifications. The blueprint that every subsequent decision references.

3 – 4 Weeks

Phase 03

Build

Design and development in parallel sprints, with weekly reviews and continuous integration. Working software over static mockups.

8 – 14 Weeks

Phase 04

Evolve

Launch support, analytics review, iteration cycles, and knowledge transfer. Systems are living — we help you grow them after handoff.

Engagement Models

Flexible by Design

We structure engagements around your reality — not ours. Three models, each calibrated for different stages of ambition.

Model A

Project

Fixed scope, fixed timeline. Ideal for defined initiatives — a rebrand, a platform build, a design system from scratch. Clear deliverables, clear milestones.

Typical duration: 8 – 20 weeks

Model B — Most Common

Retainer

Dedicated capacity on a monthly basis. Your embedded digital team — strategy, design, and engineering — available when you need them, scaling as you grow.

Typical duration: 6 – 24 months

Model C

Advisory

Strategic counsel for leadership teams navigating digital transformation. Workshops, audits, and ongoing advisory without hands-on production work.

Typical duration: Quarterly cycles
