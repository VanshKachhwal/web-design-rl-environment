What to expect

Our process after you reach out
Step 01

We reply within 24 hours

Every inquiry is read by a principal. You'll receive a direct response — not an auto-reply — confirming we've reviewed your brief.

Step 02

Discovery call (30 min)

A focused video call to understand your goals, constraints, and timeline. We'll ask sharp questions and share initial thinking on approach.

Step 03

Strategic proposal

Within one week, you receive a tailored proposal: scope, phasing, team composition, timeline, and a fixed-price investment for each phase.

Step 04

Kickoff & build

Once aligned, we mobilise within two weeks. You get a dedicated Slack channel, weekly syncs, and full transparency into every decision.
