Workshop FAQ

Do I need a conference ticket to attend workshops?

No! Workshop passes can be purchased standalone. However, conference ticket holders receive a 20% discount on all workshop add-ons.

What if a workshop is full?

Join the waitlist when registering. We typically see 2–3 cancellations per session, and waitlisted attendees are notified 48 hours before the event.

What should I wear or bring?

Movement workshops require comfortable athletic clothing. Nutrition workshops provide all materials. Digital Wellness sessions — bring your phone and laptop. Mind-Body sessions — just bring yourself and an open mind.

Can I switch workshops after registering?

Yes, switches are free up to 7 days before the event, subject to availability. Contact us at workshops@vitality2000.com.
