✦ Questions?

Registration FAQ

Can I upgrade my pass after purchasing?

Absolutely! You can upgrade at any time before the event. You'll only pay the difference between your current pass and the new tier. Email us at hello@vitality2000.com.

What is the cancellation & refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event (May 15, 2025). Between 15–30 days, you'll receive a 50% refund. Within 14 days, passes are non-refundable but fully transferable to another attendee.

Is there a group or team discount?

Yes! Groups of 2 get 5% off, 3 get 8%, 4 get 10%, and 5+ get 12% off. Select the number of attendees in the form above and the discount applies automatically at checkout.

Will sessions be recorded?

All keynotes and main-stage panels are recorded and available to every pass holder for 90 days post-event. Workshop recordings are exclusive to Vitality and Transcend pass holders.

What COVID / health safety measures are in place?

We provide hand-sanitizing stations throughout the venue, enhanced ventilation, and optional masks at registration. If you feel unwell, we offer a full transfer to next year's event at no cost.

Where is the venue & is parking available?

Vitality 2000 takes place at the Crystal Pavilion Convention Center, downtown. On-site parking is $15/day, and Transcend pass holders receive complimentary shuttle service from partner hotels.
