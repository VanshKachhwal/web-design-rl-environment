CHAPTER 01

The Origin Story

In 2022, two frustrated developers sat in a cramped apartment in Portland, staring at a terminal. They had built a CLI tool that automated database migrations — something every dev needed — but had nowhere to sell it.

App stores didn't care about terminal tools. GitHub sponsors felt like begging. Gumroad was fine for ebooks, but terrible for dev utilities that needed versioning, changelogs, and license keys.

So they built DEVSTACK: a marketplace that treats developer tools like first-class products, not afterthoughts. A place where a beautifully crafted Neovim plugin gets the same respect as a SaaS app.

What We Believe

The principles that make DEVSTACK different from every other marketplace.

>_

Devs Deserve Revenue

We take a flat 10% cut. No hidden fees, no "platform tax" that creeps up. Sellers keep 90% of every sale. Period. We publish our fee structure publicly and it hasn't changed since day one.

{ }

CLI Tools Are Products

A well-crafted terminal utility is worth paying for. We reject the idea that developer tools should always be free. Good tools save hours. Hours have value. We help devs capture that value.

◆

No Gatekeeping

No review board. No approval queue that takes 6 weeks. List your tool, set your price, start selling. We trust developers to ship quality — and buyers to leave honest reviews.

↻

Version-Aware

Unlike generic marketplaces, DEVSTACK understands semver, changelogs, and release notes. Buyers get notified of updates. Sellers can manage versions without hacking together a custom solution.

★

Community Over Hype

We don't do influencer marketing or growth hacks. Our community grows because developers tell other developers. Word of mouth is our only acquisition channel and we're proud of that.

⚡

Speed Is Respect

Our site loads in under 800ms. No cookie banners. No pop-ups begging for your email. No bloated JavaScript frameworks. We respect your time because we're developers too.

Frequently Asked

Quick answers for curious devs.

How do I start selling?

Create an account, upload your tool (ZIP, binary, or link to your repo), set a price, and you're live. No approval process. Average setup time: 8 minutes.

What's the fee structure?

Flat 10% on every sale. No monthly fees, no listing fees, no hidden charges. You make $20, you keep $18. Simple math.

Can I sell free tools?

Absolutely. Many sellers offer free tiers or open-source versions alongside paid pro editions. We support "pay what you want" pricing too.

How do payouts work?

Payouts happen every Friday via Stripe Connect. You can also choose monthly payouts if you prefer. Minimum payout threshold is $10.

Do you handle license keys?

Yes. We auto-generate unique license keys for every purchase. Buyers can manage their keys from their dashboard. You can also bring your own licensing system.

Is there a refund policy?

Buyers get a 14-day refund window. Sellers can also set custom refund policies. We handle the logistics so you don't have to deal with angry emails.
