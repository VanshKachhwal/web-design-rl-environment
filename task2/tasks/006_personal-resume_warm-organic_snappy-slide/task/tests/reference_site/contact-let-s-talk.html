How This Works

What to Expect

No bloated processes, no 47-page proposals. Here's how I work with new collaborators — lean, direct, and built for momentum.

01

You Reach Out

Send me a message through the form, email, or carrier pigeon. Tell me what you're working on and where you're stuck. The more honest, the better.

02

The Chemistry Call

A 30-minute video call where we figure out if we're a fit. I'll ask hard questions. You should too. No pitch decks — just conversation.

03

Scope & Proposal

If the energy is right, I'll send a one-page scope document with timeline, deliverables, and pricing. Clear terms, no surprises, no jargon.

04

We Make Things

The fun part. Iterative rounds, tight feedback loops, and work that pushes boundaries. I move fast and I expect the same from collaborators.
