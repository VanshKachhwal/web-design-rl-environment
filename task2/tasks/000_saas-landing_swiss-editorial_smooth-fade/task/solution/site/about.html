Our Story

From frustration to focus

2019

The spark

Co-founders Elina Strand and Marcus Voss were running a 30-person design studio drowning in tools. Three project trackers, two chat apps, one shared spreadsheet held together by hope. They decided to build the single surface they actually needed.

2020

First 500 teams

After a closed beta with Nordic startups, Clearpath launched publicly. Word spread through design and engineering communities. Zero paid ads — just teams telling other teams.

2022

Series A & global expansion

A $14M round led by Northzone let us open offices in Berlin and New York. We tripled the engineering team while keeping the product deliberately lean — adding depth, not bloat.

2024

12,000 teams and counting

Today Clearpath powers workflows at agencies, SaaS companies, and enterprise product teams across 40+ countries. Our retention rate speaks louder than any pitch deck.

Our Values

Principles we ship by

These aren't poster slogans. They're the filters every feature, hire, and decision passes through.

Clarity over cleverness

If a feature needs a tutorial, it's not ready. We optimize for instant comprehension — in the product and in every conversation.

Subtract first

The best improvement is often a removal. We regularly audit the product and cut anything that doesn't earn its pixel.

Trust the team

Autonomy with accountability. Small squads own entire surfaces end-to-end — design, code, and customer outcome.

Speed is a feature

Performance isn't polish — it's product. Every interaction in Clearpath targets sub-100ms response. Sluggish tools breed sluggish teams.

Earn attention daily

We don't lock customers in with annual contracts by default. If Clearpath stops being the best choice, you should leave. That pressure keeps us sharp.

Build in the open

Our public changelog, roadmap votes, and weekly build notes keep customers in the loop. Transparency isn't a policy — it's a habit.
