Common Questions

Before you reach out

Quick answers to the questions we hear most. If yours isn't here, use the form above.

How quickly will I hear back?

Sales inquiries receive a response within two hours during business days. Support tickets are triaged within 30 minutes for Pro and Enterprise plans.

Can I schedule a live demo?

Absolutely. Select "Request a demo" in the subject dropdown and include your preferred time. We'll send a calendar invite within one business day.

Do you offer onboarding help?

Every Pro plan includes a dedicated onboarding session. Enterprise customers get a full implementation partner assigned to their account.

What about data security?

Clearpath is SOC 2 Type II certified with end-to-end encryption. We never sell your data. Full details live on our security page.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — 14 days, no credit card required. Start from the pricing page and you'll be inside Clearpath in under two minutes.

Can I switch plans later?

Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle — no penalties, no hidden fees.
