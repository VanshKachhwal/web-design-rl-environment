Core capabilities

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Six focused tools that replace a dozen scattered apps. Built for clarity from day one.

Task boards

Drag, drop, and prioritize work across customizable columns. See every task's status at a glance without digging through menus.

Timeline view

Map dependencies and deadlines on an interactive Gantt chart. Spot bottlenecks before they stall your team.

Resource allocation

Balance workloads across your team in real time. Know who's overloaded and who has capacity — instantly.

Sprint planning

Scope sprints with story points, velocity tracking, and automated burndown charts that update as work moves forward.

Reporting dashboard

Real-time analytics on throughput, cycle time, and team health. Export to PDF or share a live link with stakeholders.

Integrations

Connect Clearpath to Slack, GitHub, Figma, and 40+ other tools your team already uses. No middleware required.
