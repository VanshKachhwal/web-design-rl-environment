FAQ

Common questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade from your account settings — changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated to the day.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes. Every new account gets a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan with full features — no credit card required.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Your data is never deleted. If you exceed the limits of a lower plan, existing items become read-only until you adjust or upgrade again.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

We offer 50% off Pro and Enterprise for verified nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Contact us to apply.

How does annual billing work?

Annual plans are billed once per year and save you 20% compared to monthly billing. You can switch to annual at any time from your billing page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH bank transfers, and wire transfers for Enterprise annual plans. All payments are processed securely.
